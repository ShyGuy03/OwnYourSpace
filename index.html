About Me

Pyscho Therapist

Barry Maloney has been studying about the human body for the last 34 years. He went from studying how we anatomically move our body, to the treatment and rehabilitation of our physical musculature. This was done through 17 years of being a Registered Massage Therapist and a personal trainer. He then did 4 years of university in Psychology to better understand what motivates us to live life as we do. Barry has graduated from the Transformational Arts College in Toronto, Ontario. He is a now a Psychotherapist (Qualifying), meaning that as a psychotherapist he is still obtaining his hours. Barry is a personal life coach as well, helping others to obtain future goals. His passion is to help others find clarity and make a positive shift in their life in order to own their own personal space.
